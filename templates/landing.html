Have a Question? There's an app for your answer!

Idea

Every thing ever invented was thought of because of a question. Here I am collecting questions which have lead you to create ideas.

Every solution is created because a problem was identified.
Here you will list all problems and their solutions, which will help us (community) create better solutions and find problems to solve.

On this site you will find questions whos answers are in the form of apps.


How to use

The Question on this site are submitted by Indie hackers, product developers and also by people who are seeking solutions for their daily problems.

After submission, each question is verified by the developer to maintain authencity of this website. Making sure each question is unique and each app is correctly placed is taken very seriously.

How is this website useful?

For Indie hackers and product developers
You can search for problems to solve here.

If you find a question related to a service you provide, you can submit your app as an answer to that question.

In addition to this, if you have already created a service, then you can submit a question whose answer is your solution and reach new audience.


For consumers
You will find multiple services and apps answering your question.

You may find solutions to your existing questions, or find an alternative to your existing solution provider.

You can also ask questions, developers and indiehackers will try to provide solutions.
