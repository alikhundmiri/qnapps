Idea

Every thing ever invented was thought of because of a question. Here I am collecting questions which have lead for you to create ideas.

How to use

There are multiple Questions whos answers are solutions in form of apps.

These questions are submitted by Indie hackers, product developers and also by people who are seeking solutions for their daily problems.

After submission, each question is verified by the developer to maintain authencity of this website. Making sure each question is unique and each app is correctly placed is taken very seriously.

How is this website useful?

For comsumers
You will find multiple services and apps suitable for your need and question.

You may find solutions to your existing questions, or find alternative to your existing solution provider.

You can even ask questions if you can't find their solutions.


For Indie hackers and product developers
You can search for problems to solve here.

If you find a question related to a service you provide, you can submit your app as an answer to that question.

In addition to this, if you have already created a service, then you can submit a question whose answer is your solution and reach new audience.
